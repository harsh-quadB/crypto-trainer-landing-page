Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: How is Crypto Trainer different from real trading apps?

A1: Unlike real trading, we give you a practice wallet with mock funds – no real money needed! Learn, make mistakes, and improve without the financial risk.

Q2: Can I really earn points and convert them into trading balance?

A2: Absolutely! As you complete lessons and courses, you’ll earn points. Reach certain milestones, and convert those points into wallet funds to practice trading with!

Q3: Are there any paid courses?

A3: Yes, some advanced courses may be paid, but most of our content is free. If you want to dive deeper, you can access premium content from Finstreet!
